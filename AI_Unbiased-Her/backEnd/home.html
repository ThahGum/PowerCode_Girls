AI UnBiased Her

The primary goal of this app is to create a positive social impact by raising awareness about gender bias and advocating for fairness and inclusivity in workplaces. The app helps companies analyze their employment data to uncover any gender imbalances and provides valuable insights to guide them in making more equitable decisions.
